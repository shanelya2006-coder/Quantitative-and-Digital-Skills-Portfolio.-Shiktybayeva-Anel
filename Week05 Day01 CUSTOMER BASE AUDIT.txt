Week05 Day01 CUSTOMER BASE AUDIT 
This project utilized quantitative and digital data analysis skills related to studying the company's customer base. A customer data set was analyzed, including determining the number of unique customers, analyzing purchasing activity, and assessing the contribution of different customers to overall sales. Calculations and data structuring were performed using spreadsheet tools, allowing for the identification of key customer behavior patterns. This project demonstrates skills in working with data, conducting basic quantitative analysis, and presenting results in a structured analytical format.
